About Me.

I was born on October 11th, 1994. I have lived my whole life in a small city in Latvia called Grobina, it is 10km away from Liepaja, the third largest city in Latvia. My hometown has around 4000 people living in it.



My childhood was mostly spent playing outside. I went to a kindergarden in my city, but always despised going there, I eventually graduated and went to my city school where I finished twelve grades.


This is me! In kindergarden!


After graduating high school, I had to choose to which university to go to. I heard about LCC from my english teacher. I liked the idea of an english languange school that had a major in business. Also, there was the benefit of living just an hour away from Klaipeda. So my last three and a half years have been spent here and it has been a great experience.

I still haven't figured out what I am going to do after finishing by Bachelors at LCC. Perhaps I'll continue to study towards a Masters degree, but who knows what'll happen.
